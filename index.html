About

Scientific devices lease/rental platform

Problem

Scientist may encounter at some part of their research the need to run an experiment that needs a specific device the University or Company he works with does not have and canâ€™t be afford.

Solution

Our solutions consist in developing an application that allows the owners of scientific devices to put their assets in lease. Scientist will search for the device based on the experiment he needs to execute, the availability of the device and how far he is from the device location. Once the scientist has found the desired device, he will book the time needed to run their experiment. The scientist can also acquire any consumable he may need to run the experiment and take any training course needed to run the device.

Business Impact

  • ThermoFisher will act as a broker, charging a fee per rent.
  • Collaboration between scientists of different institutions is promoted.
  • Scientist are no longer constrained by the devices they have access to.
  • As more experiments are executed in a device, owners will consume more often ThermoFisher consumables.
  • As scientist may need to use a device they are not familiar with, ThermoFisher training courses will be consumed by them.
